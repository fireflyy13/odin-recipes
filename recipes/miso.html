Miso

Description

Miso soup (味噌汁, misoshiru) is a traditional Japanese soup consisting of a dashi stock into which softened miso paste is mixed.

  • Water
  • Dashi
  • Tofu
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat
  2. Bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.
  4. Stir in tofu.
  5. Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup.
  6. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
